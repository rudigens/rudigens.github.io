Overview

My research specialization is in the processing and applications of synthetic aperture radar (SAR) and Interferometric SAR (InSAR) data, and their synergistic use with other remote sensing and GIS datasets. I am continuously working on a range of technical and applied research areas that I try to keep as diversified as possible. My current research efforts can be grouped into the following five thematic areas:

  • Digitial image processing for feature extraction
  • SAR, InSAR and PolSAR processing
  • Arctic and sub-Arctic applications
  • GIS for integrated analysis and change detection
  • Data visualization
